Open Data

Pulse is a project by Romario Maxwell based on the work of the General Services Administration that measures how Jamaican government domains are following best practices and it produces a lot of data in the process.

You can download that data below for reuse. It's not required, but if you use this data to do something interesting, we'd love it if you opened a ticket or dropped us a line to tell us about it!
